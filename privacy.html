Privacy Policy for IAIN Kerinci Kampus Progresif App

Last Updated: June 06, 2025

The IAIN Kerinci Kampus Progresif App ("App"), accessible at pmb.iainkerinci.ac.id, is developed and managed by IAIN Kerinci ("We," "Us," or "Our") to provide academic and registration services to students, faculty, and staff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal data, including data from Google Sign-In, when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Contact us at:
Email: datasiakad@iainkerinci.ac.id

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following to provide and improve our services:

a. Personal Information

  • Google Sign-In Data: Name, email address, and Google profile ID when you sign in with Google.
  • User Account Information: Student/staff ID, phone number, and other details provided during registration.
  • Academic and Registration Information: Course enrollment, grades, attendance, or admission-related data.
  • Authentication Data: Google ID token or other credentials for secure access.

b. Non-Personal Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and time spent on the App.
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, and access times.

c. Information Provided Voluntarily

Feedback, survey responses, or communications sent via the App or email.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate users via Google Sign-In for secure access.
  • Manage academic and registration services (e.g., course registration, admissions).
  • Send updates, announcements, or support responses.
  • Improve App functionality and user experience.
  • Comply with legal and institutional requirements.
  • Analyze usage trends for internal reporting.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share it:

  • With IAIN Kerinci Staff and Faculty: For academic and administrative purposes, such as processing registrations or managing academic records.
  • With Google: Limited data (e.g., Google ID token) is shared with Google to facilitate Sign-In, in accordance with Google’s policies.
  • With Service Providers: Trusted third parties (e.g., cloud hosting providers) who assist in operating the App and comply with data protection laws.
  • For Legal Compliance: To respond to court orders or government requests.
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to data sharing.

4. Data Storage and Security

We protect your data with:

  • Encryption for data transmission and storage.
  • Access controls for authorized personnel only.
  • Regular security assessments.

Data is stored on secure servers managed by IAIN Kerinci or trusted providers. No system is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as needed for the purposes outlined or as required by law. Academic and registration records may be kept indefinitely. Other data (e.g., logs) may be deleted per retention policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your data.
  • Correction: Fix inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion, where permitted.
  • Objection: Object to specific data uses.
  • Data Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data.

Contact datasiakad@iainkerinci.ac.id to exercise these rights.

7. Third-Party Links and Services

The App uses Google Sign-In and may link to other third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies to enhance user experience and collect usage data. Manage preferences via browser settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified via the App or email.

10. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to Indonesia or other countries, in compliance with data protection laws.

11. Children’s Privacy

The App is not for users under 17. We do not knowingly collect children’s data.
